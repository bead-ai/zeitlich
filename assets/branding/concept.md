# Zeitlich Bildmarke Concept

## Reflection

Zeitlich stands for durable AI agents that stay reliable over time, not just in ideal conditions.  
The brand should signal:

- **Time-aware execution** (Temporal DNA)
- **Resilience through failures/retries**
- **Structured orchestration** across tools and subagents
- **Clarity over chaos** in long-running agent systems

## Symbol logic

- **Broken circular arcs**: ongoing time, checkpoints, retry cycles.
- **Z-shaped resilient core**: "Zeitlich" initial, plus forward progress through interruptions.
- **Anchor nodes**: deterministic state transitions and handoffs.
- **Gradient motion** (animated version): living system, not static automation.
